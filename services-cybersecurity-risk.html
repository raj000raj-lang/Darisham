Our Process

The VAPT Process: Thinking Like the Attacker to Protect Your Core

We simulate real-world attacks on your systems so you know exactly where you’re exposed — before a real attacker finds out.

01

Scan

We simulate real-world attacks on your data centres, web portals, and infrastructure to uncover hidden exposures across your entire attack surface.

02

Identify

We manually validate each finding, stripping away false positives and categorising threats by severity — so your team knows exactly what to fix first.

03

Fortify

You receive a detailed report with actionable remediation recommendations to eliminate threats before malicious actors strike.
