Policy Research, Impact Assessment & Specialized Advisory

With over 15 years of experience across policing, IT, industries, education, cooperatives, food and dairy domains, we help clients turn policy challenges into strategic opportunities.

Policy Research & Analysis

At Darisham Consulting, we offer specialized consulting services to help our clients navigate complex policy frameworks. Our team of experts has over 15 years of experience in policy research and analysis. We work with a diverse range of domains such as policing, IT, industries, education, cooperatives, food, dairy, and more. Our policy consulting services include developing policy frameworks, policy review and evaluation, and policy impact assessment.

Whether you operate in government, private industry, or the nonprofit sector, Darisham Consulting brings deep cross-sector knowledge to deliver insights that drive meaningful and measurable impact. Partner with us to turn policy challenges into strategic opportunities.

  • Policy Development: Crafting robust, practical and forward-looking policies tailored to your organizational objectives and regulatory requirements.
  • Policy Review: Analyzing existing policies to ensure relevance, compliance and effectiveness in achieving desired outcomes.
  • Impact Assessment: Evaluating the social, economic and organizational implications of proposed or existing policies to support strategic decision-making.

Impact Assessment

At Darisham Consulting, we also excel in Impact Assessment, ensuring that our solutions have a tangible and positive effect. Through thorough evaluation and quantification, we offer clients valuable insights into the potential benefits and challenges associated with proposed changes. This commitment to a holistic approach underscores our dedication to delivering effective and sustainable solutions.

  • Social & Economic Impact Analysis: Assessing how proposed initiatives will affect communities, workforce dynamics, and economic development. We help you understand who will be impacted, how, and to what extent.
  • Policy & Regulatory Impact Studies: Evaluating the downstream effects of new or revised policies on operations, compliance, and sector-wide outcomes. Our analysis supports evidence-based policymaking and strategic planning.
  • Environmental & Sustainability Assessments: For projects with environmental dimensions, we assess ecological impacts and recommend measures to mitigate harm, ensuring alignment with sustainability goals and regulations.
  • Risk & Opportunity Evaluation: Identifying potential challenges and unintended consequences, while also highlighting opportunities for innovation, efficiency, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Stakeholder Consultation & Reporting: Facilitating transparent, inclusive assessment processes through stakeholder engagement, and delivering clear, actionable reports for decision-makers and the public.

Specialized Advisory Services

Our team of experts at Darisham Consulting provides specialized advisory services to help our clients with specific policy-related needs. Our advisory services include regulatory compliance, stakeholder engagement, and government affairs.

Compliance & Regulatory Advisory

We help clients interpret and adhere to ever-changing laws and regulations. From developing compliance frameworks to conducting audits and training, we ensure that your organization operates with integrity and minimizes legal and reputational risks.

Stakeholder Engagement Strategy

Building strong and lasting relationships with key stakeholders is essential for success. We craft tailored engagement strategies that foster trust, promote collaboration, and align stakeholder interests with organizational goals. This includes public relations support, partnership facilitation, and community outreach initiatives.

Government Affairs & Policy Advocacy

Navigating government processes and influencing public policy requires experience and tact. Our advisors work closely with clients to monitor legislative developments, prepare effective advocacy strategies, and engage decision-makers constructively to shape favorable policy outcomes.
